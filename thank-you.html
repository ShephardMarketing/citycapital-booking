How to prepare for the call

Nothing formal needed — just have a rough sense of these so we can move fast.

● Time in business

Roughly how long you've been operating. Even under 6 months can qualify.

● Monthly revenue

A ballpark of your average monthly business revenue helps us match the right program.

● Funding amount

How much capital you're looking for and what you'll use it for — inventory, payroll, growth, or paying off a higher-cost balance.

● Recent bank statements

Optional, but having your last few months of business bank statements handy can speed up approval.
